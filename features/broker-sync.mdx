## Overview

EdgeBook integrates with your brokerage account to automatically import your trades and orders. No more manual data entry - your executions are synced directly from your broker.

<Note>
  Broker sync is powered by [SnapTrade](https://snaptrade.com), a secure financial data aggregator that connects to 50+ brokers.
</Note>

## Connecting Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click your profile menu and select **Settings**, then navigate to the **Broker Connections** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Connection">
    Click **Connect Broker** to open the SnapTrade connection flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Broker">
    Find and select your broker from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Log in to your broker account and authorize EdgeBook to access your trade data. This is read-only access - EdgeBook cannot place trades.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Accounts">
    Choose which accounts to sync. You can connect multiple accounts from the same broker.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Synced Data

Once connected, EdgeBook imports:

### Trades
- Symbol and direction
- Entry and exit prices
- Quantity and position size
- Execution timestamps
- Realized P&L

### Orders
- Order type (market, limit, stop)
- Fill prices and quantities
- Order status
- Timestamps

## Viewing Broker Data

### Orders View

Access imported orders from the **Orders** tab in the sidebar:

- View all executions across connected accounts
- Filter by account, symbol, or date
- See detailed order information

### Linking to Journal Entries

Connect broker-imported trades to your journal entries:

1. Open a trade in your journal
2. Click **Link Broker Trade**
3. Select the matching execution from your broker data
4. The trade is now linked with accurate P&L data

## Account Management

### Multiple Accounts

You can connect multiple brokerage accounts:
- Different brokers
- Multiple accounts at the same broker
- Paper trading accounts

### Filtering by Account

Use the account filter in the Orders view to see trades from specific accounts.

### Disconnecting

To remove a broker connection:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Broker Connections**
2. Click the disconnect button next to the account
3. Confirm the disconnection

<Warning>
  Disconnecting removes access to future data. Previously imported trades remain in your journal.
</Warning>

## Data Security

Your broker credentials are never stored by EdgeBook:

- **OAuth authentication** - You log in directly with your broker
- **Read-only access** - EdgeBook can only view data, not place trades
- **Encrypted connections** - All data transfer is encrypted
- **SnapTrade security** - Bank-level security standards

## Sync Frequency

Broker data is synced:
- When you first connect
- Periodically in the background
- When you manually refresh

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connection failed" icon="circle-xmark">
    Try disconnecting and reconnecting your broker. Some brokers require re-authentication periodically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Missing trades" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Broker data may take a few minutes to sync. Try refreshing the page or wait for the next sync cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Wrong account" icon="building-columns">
    Make sure you selected the correct account during setup. You can connect additional accounts from Settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
