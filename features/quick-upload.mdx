## Drag & Drop Upload

The fastest way to log a trade is to drag a screenshot directly onto EdgeBook.

### How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Drag any image">
    Grab a screenshot from your desktop, Finder, or downloads folder
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drop onto EdgeBook">
    A green overlay appears showing "Drop to create new trade"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    The New Trade form opens with your image already attached. Add symbol, notes, and trade details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your trade is logged with the chart screenshot
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Supported formats

- PNG
- JPG/JPEG
- WEBP
- GIF

### Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Multiple images" icon="images">
    Drag multiple screenshots at once to attach them all to a single trade
  </Card>
  <Card title="First image = thumbnail" icon="image">
    The first image you drop becomes the trade's thumbnail in the gallery
  </Card>
  <Card title="Works anywhere" icon="bullseye">
    Drop images anywhere on the app window - not just the upload area
  </Card>
  <Card title="From any source" icon="folder-open">
    Works with files from desktop, Finder, file browsers, or even other apps
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Keyboard shortcuts

While the upload modal is open:

| Action | Shortcut |
|--------|----------|
| Remove selected image | `Delete` or `Backspace` |
| Navigate images | Arrow keys |
| Confirm upload | `Enter` |
| Cancel | `Escape` |
