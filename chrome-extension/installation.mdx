## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Chrome Web Store">
    Go to the [EdgeBook Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore) on the Chrome Web Store.

    <Note>
      Search for "EdgeBook" or "EdgeBook Trading Journal" if the direct link doesn't work.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Add to Chrome">
    Click the **Add to Chrome** button on the extension page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Installation">
    A popup will ask for permissions. Click **Add extension** to confirm.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pin the Extension">
    Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar, then click the pin icon next to EdgeBook.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First-Time Setup

After installation, you need to connect your EdgeBook account:

1. Click the EdgeBook icon in your toolbar
2. Click **Sign In**
3. Log in with your EdgeBook credentials
4. The extension is now connected

<Tip>
  You only need to sign in once. The extension remembers your session.
</Tip>

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests these permissions:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Read TradingView** | To detect the current symbol and capture charts |
| **Storage** | To save your preferences and session |
| **Tabs** | To know when you're on TradingView |

<Note>
  The extension can only read data from TradingView pages - it cannot access any other websites.
</Note>

## Using the Extension

### Capturing a Trade

1. Open any chart on TradingView
2. Set up your chart with annotations, drawings, indicators
3. Click the EdgeBook extension icon
4. Your chart is automatically screenshotted
5. Fill in the trade details:
   - Direction (Long/Short)
   - Entry price
   - Stop loss / Take profit
   - Notes and tags
6. Click **Save Trade**

### Quick Capture vs Full Form

**Quick Capture** (default):
- Screenshot + Symbol only
- Add details later in EdgeBook

**Full Form**:
- Fill all details in the popup
- More complete trade record

Toggle between modes in extension settings.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Extension not showing on TradingView" icon="eye-slash">
    1. Make sure you're on `tradingview.com`
    2. Refresh the page
    3. Check if the extension is enabled in `chrome://extensions`
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Screenshot is blank or wrong" icon="image">
    1. Wait for the chart to fully load
    2. Make sure no dialogs or popups are open
    3. Try zooming out if the chart is very large
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't sign in" icon="lock">
    1. Check your EdgeBook credentials at edgebook.io
    2. Clear extension data and try again
    3. Make sure you have an active subscription
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Trade not appearing in EdgeBook" icon="cloud-xmark">
    1. Check your internet connection
    2. Verify you're signed into the extension
    3. Look in the "Recent" or "All" view - might be filtered out
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Symbol not auto-detected" icon="magnifying-glass">
    1. Make sure a chart is open (not just the homepage)
    2. The symbol appears in TradingView's title bar
    3. Try refreshing the page
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Updating the Extension

Chrome automatically updates extensions. To manually check:

1. Go to `chrome://extensions`
2. Enable **Developer mode** (top right)
3. Click **Update**

## Uninstalling

If you need to remove the extension:

1. Right-click the EdgeBook icon in your toolbar
2. Select **Remove from Chrome**
3. Confirm removal

Your trades in EdgeBook are not affected - they remain in your account.
