## Interface Overview

EdgeBook's interface is organized into several key areas:

### Sidebar Navigation
- **Trades** - Your main trade journal
- **Patterns** - Your pattern library
- **Strategies** - Strategy and setup management
- **Orders** - Broker-imported executions
- **Manage** - Tags, statuses, symbols, and more

### Trade Views
Switch between different views to organize your trades:
- **List View** - Card-based gallery of trades
- **Board View** - Kanban-style workflow management
- **Table View** - Spreadsheet-style data view

### Filtering
Use the sidebar filters to find specific trades by:
- Result (Win/Loss/Breakeven)
- Direction (Long/Short)
- Status, Tags, Symbols
- Date range
- Rating

<Tip>
  **Pro tip:** Save your favorite filter combinations as "Saved Views" for one-click access.
</Tip>
